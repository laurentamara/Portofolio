About

2025

Molecular Docking on HMPV Virus

Conducted molecular docking simulations to explore potential interactions between Indonesian herbal compounds and the SH and G proteins of Human Metapneumovirus (HMPV). The workflow included protein structure retrieval, ligand preparation, and docking analysis using AutoDock Vina. The project aimed to identify candidate compounds with promising binding affinity for further in-vitro or in-silico validation.
